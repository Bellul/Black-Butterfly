RESOURCES

Suicide is becoming shockingly prevalent in the young black community. From the lack of resources and openness in the black community, there needs to be a shift in willinglness to be vulnerabile to avoid the rise in deaths. It is imperative that there is a platform that allows open communication within the community to share their specific experiences, as Black people, to prevent a lifelong burden. It's important to be informed and educated in these topics. Here you can find multiple resources and hotlines to learn more about suicide in the black community.

Black and African American Suicide Prevention

Visit this website to learn more about the details of suicide rates within the black community. There are also warning signs and tips for self-care and how to help a friend in need.

Black Mental Health Alliance

The Black Mental Health Alliance is an organization that connects individuals with therapists and provides professional training. You can find information about the community stigmas and different alliances open for all the join. Click the title to learn how to connec to a therapist.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Call 800-273-8255 if you ever feel in distress and need someone to talk to.

Suicide Prevention Resource Center

Visit this website to be informed about about the statistics of rates for black suicide in comparison to other races, and sex.


About us

Black Butterfly is a suicide prevention nonprofit. Our goal? To bring awareness to the rising rates of suicide within the black community.
